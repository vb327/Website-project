I read all of the books when I was in second grade, I watched all of the movies in the movie thater. I have always wanted the same kind of friendship as Harry, Ron, and Hermione had. There is a Broadway play that is kind of a sequal to the books called Harry Potter and the Cursed Child; I saw it a few months ago and it was amazing! These books are a huge part of my childhood and I could not be happier that I was born at the time when the books were being written!
